# Evidence Definition

## What Is Evidence?

Evidence is a verifiable observation extracted from a source that may support, contradict, or provide context for a higher-level conclusion.

Evidence is not a trend.

Evidence is not a friction.

Evidence is not a recommendation.

Evidence is not an opportunity.

Evidence is the raw material from which those conclusions may eventually emerge.

---

## Core Definition

An evidence record should answer:

* What was observed?
* Where was it observed?
* When was it observed?
* Who observed it?
* What exact source supports it?

If those questions cannot be answered, the record is not evidence.

---

## Evidence Must Be Verifiable

Every evidence record must be traceable back to a source.

Examples:

* Reddit post
* GitHub issue
* Hacker News discussion
* Research paper
* Product review
* Forum discussion
* Support ticket
* Documentation issue

Evidence without a source is not valid evidence.

---

## Evidence Is Not A Conclusion

Evidence:

"Spent three hours configuring OAuth."

Conclusion:

"OAuth onboarding is complex."

The first is evidence.

The second is an interpretation built from evidence.

---

## Evidence Is Not A Score

Evidence should never contain:

* Confidence scores
* Opportunity scores
* Friction scores
* Trend scores
* AI certainty estimates

Evidence stores observations.

Humans determine meaning.

---

## Evidence Is Atomic

A single source may contain multiple evidence records.

Example:

Post:

"Spent three hours configuring OAuth. Ended up writing a custom script because the documentation was confusing."

Possible evidence:

1. Time spent configuring OAuth.
2. Custom workaround created.
3. Documentation confusion.

One post.
Three evidence records.

---

## Evidence Is Neutral

Evidence may support a claim.

Evidence may contradict a claim.

Evidence may provide context.

The platform stores observations, not conclusions.

---

## Evidence Can Support Multiple Objects

The same evidence may support:

* A trend
* A friction
* A research theme
* A market observation

Evidence should remain reusable.

---

## Evidence Is Immutable

The original observation should never be modified.

Additional interpretation may be added later.

The original evidence remains unchanged.

---

## Evidence Exists Before Intelligence

The platform flow is:

Post
→ Evidence
→ Validation
→ Intelligence Object

Evidence is the foundation of the platform.
